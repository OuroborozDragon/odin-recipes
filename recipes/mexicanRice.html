Mexican Rice

Ingredients

  • One cup of Jasmine rice or 4 fist-fulls
  • 2 Tomatoes
  • 2 Cloves of garlic
  • 1/4 of an onion
  • Chicken bouillon
  • Vegetable Oil

Steps:

  1. Add 2 tablespoon of oil to a pan.
  2. Add rice to the pan and cook until lightly brown.
  3. In the meantime cut the onion and tomatoes and add them to the blender along with the garlic cloves.
  4. Once fully blended strain it into the pan of lightly browned rice.
  5. Add chicken bouillon to taste
  6. Add 3 cups of water
  7. Once it starts to boil cover it with a lid and set the flame to medium.
