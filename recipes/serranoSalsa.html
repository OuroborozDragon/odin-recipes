Spicy Serrano Salsa

Ingredients

  • 6 Green tomatillos
  • 3 Serrano Chiles
  • 1/4 White Onion
  • 2 or 3 Garlic Cloves
  • A handful of cilantro
  • Salt

Steps:

  1. Peel the green tomatillos and clean them in the sink with water.
  2. Remove the stem from the chiles and rinse the chiles in the sink with water
  3. Make sure to remove all stickly residue from the vegetables
  4. Boil both the tomatillos and the chiles in a pot until they fully change color and soften
  5. Drain the pot once they have changed color and add the vegetables to a blender

    6. (waiting for them to cool down first is optional)

  6. Blend the tomatillos, chiles, gralic, onion, cilantro, and salt to taste
  7. Add the freshly blended salsa to a container and it's done!
